The air of tomorrow

What is Air Pollution? The presence of toxic chemicals or compounds that create health risks for living organisms.

How is Air Pollution produced? Mostly due to human activities such as drilling for fossil fuels, refining the fuels, and their use in society such as emissions.

Bay Area is home to various oil refineries. Three oil refineries in the East Bay are: Richmond (Chevron Refinery), Martinez (Shell Refinery), Rodeo (Phillips 66),

Our Users Needs were: the need for parents to be aware that air quality is bad in their area, the need for education about air quality, and the need for more health care.

Our own Insights: UCSF has a medical legal program & works with the county on the Asthma Starts program Education on wellness management can greatly improve the situation of those with respiratory issues caused by their environment.

We came up with many ideas to help solve the needs of our user group such as: A community Board, An app Refreshers (which are nose air filters), and a wellness kit.
